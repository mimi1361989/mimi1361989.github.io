Since Mimi has completed a postgraduate award in the University of Queensland recognised by CPA Australia (Certified Practising Accountants), she was qualified as an Associate Member of this accounting body.

Mimi has enrolled in the Master of Commerce Program, which is designed for people aspiring to financial management roles. This was an opportunity for Mimi to apply her university learning in a real corporate environment, acquire new skills, and grow her industry network.
